1. ANDRO ENGINE Workshop

National level championship and a workshop organised by Techtron Technologies.

In association with Elan 2017, Indian Institute of Technology, Hyderabad and Assam Don Bosco University, Airport Road, Azara.

The workshop is on "Andro-Engine" which will comprise of various sessions. The workshop is a combination of various branches like Mechanical Engineering, Electronics and Communication Engineering, Chemical Engineering, Computer Science Engineering and Information Technology.

In the workshop the automobile engine will be controlled by an Android Phone using a developer base and Arduino microcontroller moreover two 4 stroke engines will be overhaul.

  1. 2 cylinder 4 stroke Engine
  2. 3 cylinder 4 stroke Engine

This workshop will be first time held in India, no other institution or any research organisation ever conducted a workshop on this topic. It's a new technology which might be in vast and commercial use in near future. Instead of a key an application file(mobile app) will be used to ignite the engine.

The national level championship is also a main part of the workshop. In the championship students will be judged on basis of their practical skills and theoretical knowledge. The student who so ever is selected by the resource person present in the Workshop will directly get qualified to attend the last round of the championship at I.I.T, Hyderabad.

The questions in the championship will be asked from the course content of the workshop, this will also be done to check the knowledge acquired by the student in the workshop.
