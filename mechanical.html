Autocad Competition

No. of participants: 1

On-Spot Registration: Rs. 50

  • Each participant will be provided with 3-4 hours to finish their respective models.
  • Each participants will be provided with one single computer.
  • The participants will have to perform the modeling in Autodesk, Autocad 2014.
  • All the dimensions will be provided in mm (millimeter).
  • Drawing problems (for 2D) will be judged on completeness, accuracy, line quality, lettering and neatness. CAD modeling problems (for 3D) will be judged on completeness, accuracy, line type and dimensioning and with various “spot-check” rubrics.
  • Any measuring instrument or calculator is not allowed during the competition runtime.

      • Hosted by: Mechanical Club
