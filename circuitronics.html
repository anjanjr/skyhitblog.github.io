1. Circuit Debugging

Tentative time limit:

​

​Prelims: 30 min

​​

Mains: 1 hr

General rules (tentative):

  1. Group of maximum 3 members.
  3. Preliminary round will consist of objective Q/A. Negative marking for wrong answer.
  4. No calculators are allowed. Extra sheets for rough work shall be provided.
  5. 10 groups (max) will be selected to the mains round.
  6. Mains round will constitute debugging a circuit which has some erroneous connections. Participants will be given a time limit inside which they have to identify the errors and make necessary corrections to the circuit.

2. Labyrinth

Tentative time limit:

​​

Prelims: 1 hr

​​

Mains: 1hr 30 min

General Rules (tentative):

  1. Group of max 3 members.
  2. In prelims, participants will be asked to design a simple circuit, e.g., a full wave bridge rectifier.
  3. 10 groups (max) will be selected for the mains round.
  4. If more than 10 groups execute the circuit, speed of execution will be taken into consideration.
  5. In mains, a set of components will be given. Participants will have to use their creativity and design a circuit.
