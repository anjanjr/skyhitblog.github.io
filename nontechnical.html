1. Extempore speech

Registration fees: ₹20

  1. Each participant will get two topics from a box of topics.
  2. The primary topic will be carrying more points than the secondary.
  3. There will be a limited time for each participant.
  4. There will be no points if the participants continue his/her speech after the final bell.
Prizes Worth: 1.5K

2. Paper presentation

Registration fees: ₹30

  1. Students will have to prepare a powerpoint presentation on the topics given below.
  2. Use standard font (Times New Roman, Calibri, etc ), slide number, college logo, content page, references, etc.
  3. Background of the presentation should be clear and readable.
  4. The maximum no. of slides will be 13 (including slide no., topic name, content, etc).
  5. The maximum time for each group will be 10min.
  6. There will be final and warning bell for each group.

  2. Electronic noise
  3. Wireless technology
  4. Nature inspired computing
  5. Speech and language processing for human machine communication
  6. Next generation network
  7. Rocket science
  8. Nano materials
  9. Numerical methods
  10. Eigen values and Eigen vectors and its application
  11. Renewable energy and environmental protection (for example : renewable energy from land farm, solar, etc)
  12. Bio battery
  13. E-Commerce
  14. Smart Dust
  15. 4G Wireless Systems
Prizes Worth: 3.5K

Hosted by: Basic Sciences Club

3. Debate Competition

  3. Time limit will be (3+1) minutes
  4. First speaker gets in total of 5 minutes. That is (3+2)
  5. And also an additional of 2 mins at the end to conclude the debate for which he/she won't be judged.
