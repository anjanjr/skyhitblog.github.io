1. Extempore speech

Registration fees: ₹20

Rules :

  1. Each participant will get two topics from a set of topics.
  2. The primary topic will carry more points than the secondary.
  3. There will be time limit for each participant.
  4. There will be no points if the participant continues his/her speech after the final bell.
Prizes Worth: 1.5K

Hosted by: Basic Sciences Club

2. Paper presentation

Registration fees: ₹30

Group Event of 2 Members.

Rules :

  1. The teams will have to prepare a powerpoint presentation on any one of the topics given below.
  2. Standard font (Times New Roman, Calibri, etc.) has be used.
  3. The presentation should include slide number, college logo, content page and references.
  4. Background of the presentation should be clear and readable.
  5. The maximum no. of slides is 13 (inclusive of all slides).
  6. The maximum time allotted to each group is 10 minutes.
  7. Decision of the judges will be final.

The topics for the Paper Presentation:

  1. Wearable bio-sensor
  2. Electronic noise
  3. Wireless technology
  4. Nature inspired computing
  5. Speech and language processing for human machine communication
  6. Next generation network
  7. Rocket science
  8. Nano materials
  9. Numerical methods
  10. Eigen values and Eigen vectors and its application
  11. Renewable energy and environmental protection (for example : renewable energy from land farm, solar, etc)
  12. Bio battery
  13. E-Commerce
  14. Smart Dust
  15. 4G Wireless Systems
Prizes Worth: 3.5K

Hosted by: Basic Sciences Club
